Privacy Policy

The Monster Math respects your privacy.

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app does not require accounts, logins, or internet connectivity to function.

All progress and game data are stored locally on the user's device.

The Monster Math does not include:

  • Advertising
  • Analytics tracking
  • Third-party data collection
  • External user accounts
