Work In Progress

A unified commerce and visibility platform for high schools.

Native Dev is actively designing a school-focused platform that brings together point of sale, event ticketing, rewards, fundraising, inventory, digital payments, and reporting into one connected system.

The goal is not just to process transactions, but to make it easy for school staff to instantly understand what sold, what is left, what events performed well, and what needs attention next.

What it is for

The platform is meant to replace the patchwork of tools schools often use for student purchases, event access, school stores, fundraising, and spreadsheet-heavy reporting.

Who it serves

Students use it to buy, attend, and earn rewards. Staff use it to manage sales, events, inventory, and reporting. Administrators get clearer financial and operational visibility without needing technical expertise.

What makes it different

It is being designed specifically for high school environments, where student identity, school events, store sales, and day-to-day operational visibility all intersect.

Reporting And Visibility

Reporting is a core feature, not an afterthought.

Staff should be able to log in and answer key questions in seconds, without exporting raw data and rebuilding the story in spreadsheets.

Sales Clarity

See total sales, gross vs net, taxes collected, payment breakdowns, and category performance by day, week, month, event, or custom range.

Inventory Visibility

Quickly understand what products sell most, what is not moving, what inventory remains, and which items need attention before they cause problems.

Student And Event Insight

Track who is buying, who is most engaged, what rewards are being used, how events are performing, and how ticketing activity translates into participation.

Core Areas

One system, several connected jobs.

Commerce

Point of sale, digital payments, inventory, order tracking, school-store operations, and commerce records in one place.

Events

Ticket sales, validation, attendance visibility, and event performance reporting for games, dances, performances, and other school activities.

Engagement

Student identity, loyalty, rewards, promotions, and student-facing experiences designed to increase participation across school life.
