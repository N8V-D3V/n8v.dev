Work In Progress

A unified commerce and engagement platform for high schools.

Native Dev is actively designing a school-focused platform that brings together point of sale, event ticketing, rewards, fundraising, inventory, and digital payments into one connected system.

What it is for

The goal is to replace the patchwork of tools schools often use for student purchases, event access, school stores, and fundraising with a simpler, more unified system.

Who it serves

Students use it to buy, attend, and earn rewards. Staff use it to manage sales, events, inventory, and reporting. Administrators get a clearer view of the overall system.

What makes it different

It is being designed specifically for high school environments, where student identity, school events, and everyday purchases all intersect.

Core Areas

One system, several connected jobs.

Commerce

Point of sale, digital payments, inventory, order tracking, reporting, and school-store operations in one place.

Events

Ticket sales, validation, and access management for games, dances, performances, and other school activities.

Engagement

Student identity, loyalty, rewards, and incentives designed to increase participation across school life.
