Privacy Policy

The Monster Match respects your privacy and keeps the experience lightweight, simple, and local to the device.

What We Do

This app does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The app does not require accounts, logins, or internet connectivity to function.

All progress and game data are stored locally on the player's device.

What The App Does Not Include

  • Advertising
  • Analytics tracking
  • Third-party data collection
  • External user accounts
Questions about this policy can be sent to support@n8v.dev.
