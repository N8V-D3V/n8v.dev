Privacy Policy

The Monster Match does not collect personal data. Game progress stays on the device, and no account is required to play.

How Privacy Works

This app does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The app does not require accounts, logins, or a persistent internet connection to function.

Game progress and related data are stored locally on the player's device.

What The App Does Not Use

  • Advertising
  • Analytics tracking
  • Third-party data collection
  • External user accounts
Questions about this policy or the game can be sent to support@n8v.dev.
