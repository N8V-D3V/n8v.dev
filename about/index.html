About Native Dev

Built by a mobile engineer with a founder-builder mindset.

N8V D3V is a studio home for Native Dev games, experiments, and future software projects. It reflects a path from small-town creative work to large-scale mobile systems used in major sports and entertainment venues.

ᏣᎳᎩ | The Native Dev, LLC

Creative roots

The story starts in small-town rural Utah, where a concurrent-enrollment multimedia program opened the door to design, video, and creative tools. Even early on, there was a habit of teaching new skills on the fly to bring ideas to life.

Choosing software

After a short detour into social media marketing, it became clear that the ideas worth chasing were not campaigns. They were apps he wished existed. That shift led to Full Sail's mobile development program and a move to Florida with his wife and young family.

Building at scale

Before graduation, the founder started creating opportunities by writing letters, visiting local tech teams, and building real relationships. That persistence led to years of work on mobile systems, SDKs, and hardware-heavy apps used across major sports and entertainment venues.

Builder Mindset

What shapes the work today.

The same background that helped build large-scale mobile systems now shapes how Native Dev approaches products: clearer customer experience, intuitive flows, and software that holds up in the real world.

Customer Experience

Years of shipping public-facing mobile products created a strong bias toward clarity, ease of use, and reducing friction for the people actually using the software.

Systems Thinking

Experience with SDKs, hardware integrations, and high-reliability apps built an instinct for modular architecture and tools other teams can rely on.

Founder Builder

N8V D3V is where that experience turns into original games, new ideas, and software projects that can grow into something bigger over time.

N8V D3V is where creator instincts, large-scale mobile experience, and a builder's eye for what comes next come together.
